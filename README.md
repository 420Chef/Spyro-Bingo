### **Spyro Bingo: How to Play**

---

### **1. Pick Your Mode**
* **Solo:** You vs. The Clock.
* **Multiplayer:** You vs. A Friend.

---

### **2. How it Works**
* **The Board:** You get a grid of random Spyro tasks (e.g., "Burn the pink tulip").
* **The Start:** Click the middle square (**NESTOR SKIP**) to start the timer.
* **Marking:** Click a square when you finish that task in the game.
* **Winning:**
    * **Solo:** Get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
    * **Multiplayer (Lockout):** Be the first to claim **13 squares** total. If you take a square, your friend can't have it!

---

### **3. Cool Features**
* **Edit Tasks:** Want different goals? Click **Edit Tasks** and type in whatever you want.
* **Save Your Win:** Click **Save Result** at the end to get a picture of your board and time.
* **Play Anywhere:** Works on your phone while you play the game on TV.

---

### **4. Setting up Multiplayer**
1.  **Host:** Give your friend your **ID code**.
2.  **Guest:** Type that code in and hit **Connect**.
3.  **Play:** The board stays synced for both of you automatically.

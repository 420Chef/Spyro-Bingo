💎 Spyro Challenge Bingo
A simple, interactive Bingo card for Spyro the Dragon challenge runs.

What it is|
Randomized Boards: Generates a new grid from 50 unique Spyro tasks every time you shuffle.

Built-in Timer: Tracks your speedrun progress from the moment you start.

Mobile Friendly: Designed to look great on your phone while you play.

How to Use|
Start: Tap the Gold Free Space in the center to start the timer.

Mark: Tap any square to mark it as complete.

Win: Get 5 in a row (any direction) to stop the clock and trigger the celebration.

Reset: Hit Shuffle to clear the board and reset the timer for a new run.

Customizing|
Edit Tasks: Tap the Edit Tasks button to paste in your own custom list of goals.

Dark Mode: Switch themes for better visibility during late-night gaming.
